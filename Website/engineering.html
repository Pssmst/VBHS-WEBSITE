Pre-Engineering Pathway

Learn CAD, build robots, program CNC mills, and design your own capstone prototype.

Course Sequence

  • 9th Grade

    Introduction to Engineering Design (IED)

    Hands-on CAD & design process projects: puzzle cubes, Automoblox, cable cars…

  • 10th Grade

    Principles of Engineering (POE)

    Survey major engineering topics: mechanisms, energy, statics, materials…

  • 11th Grade

    Computer-Integrated Manufacturing (CIM)

    CAD/CAM, CNC mills & lathes, VEX robotics, virtual manufacturing badge.

  • 12th Grade

    Engineering Design & Development (EDD)

    Capstone: team-based open-ended problem, prototype, mentor feedback, panel defense.
